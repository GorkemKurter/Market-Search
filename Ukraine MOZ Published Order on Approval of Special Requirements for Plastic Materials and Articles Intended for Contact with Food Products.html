SUMMARY

On December 11, 2023, the Ministry of Health (MOZ) published an order on approval of special requirements for plastic materials and articles intended for contact with food products.


DETAILS

The order specifies guidelines for plastic materials and objects that come into contact with food implementing the European Commission Regulation No. 10/2011 regarding plastic materials and articles intended for food contact This Order approves the Special Requirements below for plastic materials and articles intended for contact with food products and establishes that products made of plastic materials and articles intended for contact with food products, which were produced before the entry into force of this order, can be in circulation until the end of their shelf. This Order also discusses the provisions related to: • Requirements for the composition of plastic materials and objects intended for contact with food products (Section II): • General requirements, limitations and technical characteristics (Section III) • Special provisions for multi-layered plastic materials or articles (Section IV) • Special provisions for layered materials or articles (Section V) Annexes: Annex 1 (under paragraph 1 of Section II) lists the permitted substances: The list of permitted monomers, other starting substances, macromolecules obtained during microbial fermentation, additives and auxiliary means for the production of polymers is given in Table 1; Information on group restrictions of substances is given in Table 2; Compliance check notes are provided in Table 3; and Information on the detailed specification of substances is given in Table 4. Annex 2 (under paragraph 3 of Section II) specifies the general list of migration limits for migrating substances from plastic materials and articles Annex 3 (under sub-clause 3 of clause 4 of Section III) lists the primary aromatic amines. This Order enters into force simultaneously with the entry into force of the Law of Ukraine "On Materials and Articles Intended to Come into Contact with Food", on November 19, 2025.

AFFECTED PRODUCTS DETAILS

Plastic materials and articles intended for contact with food belonging to the following categories: materials and objects and their parts consisting exclusively of plastic; plastic multi-layer materials and objects that are attached to using glue or other methods; materials and items specified in subparagraphs 1 or 2 printed and/or coated; layers of plastic or plastic coatings forming gaskets in caps and plugs, which together with these caps and plugs form a set of two or more layers of different types of materials; and layers of plastic in multilayer materials and objects. Exemptions: ion exchange resins; rubber silicones printing inks glues varnishes and coatings.

IMPACT TO CONFORMITY ASSESSMENT PROCESS

N/A

N/A

IMPACT TO TECHNICAL REQUIREMENT

Starting November 11, 2025, plastic materials and articles in contact with food must comply with the technical requirements established under this Order, such as composition and migration requirements. The order is alligned with EU Regulation 10/2011 on plastic materials and articles.
